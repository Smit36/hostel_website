how do you get here

arriving by train

AVD campus is around 7 Km away from Anand railway station. Pick up an auto/bus and drive via GJ SH 60 & 100 Feet Rd or via Anand-Vidyanagar Road leading to AVD Campus.

arriving by bus

New Anand Bus Station - 6km away from campus. Easily commute by public transport which travels through Zydus Hospital and then pick GJ SH 60 Rd leading to Campus.

Personal Transports

Vehicles arriving from NH-8 need to pass through Samarkha Chokdi and drive through Bhalej road leading to 100 Feet rd which leads to campus.
